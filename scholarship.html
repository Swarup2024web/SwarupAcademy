Scholarship Program

We are proud to support bright and needy students. If you meet the criteria below, we encourage you to apply for the Swarup Academy Scholarship.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed all subjects in your previous class
  • Obtained 80% overall
  • Achieved at least 60% in both Mathematics and English
  • Must be a student of Swarup Academy
  • Limited to students who have passed Class 8 or Class 9
